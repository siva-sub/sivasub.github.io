What the sources actually support

The microsite now uses primary operator and standards sources first. Newsletters and media are trend signals only.

Stripe

SPTs are scoped grants with usage limits, expiry, revocation, and step-up actions such as 3DS.

Visa

Payment instructions, agent-specific tokens, network controls, and commerce signals connect authorization back to intent.

Mastercard

Agent Pay adds agentic tokens and identifiers so network participants can recognize agent-led transactions.

BIS CPMI

Fast payments change recourse because payees can receive final, irrevocable funds before or alongside inter-PSP settlement.

Claim hygiene

Say this carefully

The stronger post is the one that preserves uncertainty where the sources do.

Safe

“The industry pattern is scoped credentials and auditable instructions, not handing an AI agent unrestricted payment access.”

Safe

“Instant bank rails narrow the after-the-fact reversal path, so authority checks move earlier in the flow.”

Qualify

“Legal liability for agent mistakes is still being defined. Technical audit trails help, but they do not settle every legal edge case.”

Do not say

“Stablecoins solve agentic payments.” The supported claim is narrower: they move the control problem to wallet authority, compliance, redemption, and finality.
